Diaries Of The Young Adult

What is it that fuels you? For me, it’s the strength that I derive when I look back at how much I have grown, how much I have overcome in the so many wins in the smallest ways as a young woman. I have always been creative but I also wanted to engage others. I believed establishing a site that was accessible to many and that allowed them to express themselves as young and zealous girls developing into adults they want to be was a great way to share stories, experiences, and inspiration with others. The Diaries Of The Young Adult is truly my own little passion project, gaining more and more traction each day. I hope you enjoy browsing my site and all of the unique content I have to offer. Take a look around; perhaps you’ll discover what fuels you as well. Read on and enjoy the many stories and creative expressions from girls all around the world!

Stories Around Us

My name is Faith Sampaya, I am currently doing a course in nursing. Growing up I didn’t have it easy and I am honestly grateful to God for looking at where I am today.  I did fail my first year of nursing school and I felt crushed that the person I really wanted to be wasn’t possible. I felt ashamed and scared to face the family members who have been taking care of my school fees. Nights and days went by and I felt the weight of repeating my first year. It took a lot to not give up,It took a lot to work on the bunch of workloads and it took a lot of newfound determination and discipline to fight for the person I want to be.I hope you too find that and keep fighting for the future you!
